About Me

Hi! I am Harshita Jogi, someone who has always been curious about how things work, whether it was dismantling gadgets as a child or understanding why an algorithm makes a certain decision today. That same curiosity now drives me to build technology that feels thoughtful, intuitive, and human.

I grew up in a family that valued hard work and quiet perseverance, and as a first-generation engineer, I have learned to carve my own path: one that blends logic with creativity. Whether it's designing a drone model, simplifying data systems, or refining an idea until it just clicks, I find joy in the process of learning and creating.

Outside work, I love art, music, and nature: things that remind me there is rhythm and balance even in complexity. Years of elocution training from Trinity College London and Bharatnatyam have taught me expression and discipline, qualities I carry into every project I take on.
